## Question: Who is known as the Father of Afghanistan?

### Short Answer
**Ahmad Shah Durrani** is known as the Father of Afghanistan.

### Detailed Explanation

Ahmad Shah Durrani (1722-1772), originally named Ahmad Khan Abdali, is universally recognized as the **Father of Afghanistan** and the founder of the modern Afghan nation.

#### Key Facts About Ahmad Shah Durrani

1. **Founding of Afghanistan (1747)**
   - Established the Durrani Empire in 1747 in Kandahar
   - Unified various Pashtun tribes under one banner
   - Created the first unified Afghan state

2. **Military Achievements**
   - Won the Third Battle of Panipat (1761) against the Marathas
   - Extended Afghan territory from Khorasan to Delhi
   - Controlled Kashmir, Punjab, and Sindh

3. **Administrative Reforms**
   - Created a federal structure respecting tribal autonomy
   - Established Kandahar and later Kabul as capitals
   - Promoted trade and economic development

4. **Legacy**
   - Laid the foundation for modern Afghanistan's boundaries
   - Respected by all Afghan ethnic groups
   - His mausoleum in Kandahar remains a national shrine
