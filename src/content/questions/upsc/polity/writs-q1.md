question: "The writ of 'Habeas Corpus' is issued to:"
options:
  - "Prevent a person from holding a public office"
  - "Order a public official to perform their legal duty"
  - "Produce a person before the court and release them if detention is illegal"
  - "Transfer a case from lower court to higher court"
correctAnswer: 2
explanation: "Habeas Corpus literally means 'to have the body of'. It is a writ issued by the court to produce a person who has been detained, whether in prison or in private custody, before the court and to release them if the detention is found to be illegal. This writ is the bulwark of individual liberty against arbitrary detention. It can be issued against both public and private individuals."
